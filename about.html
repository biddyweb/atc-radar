Open Air Data

The Open Air Data project's goal is to create open repositories of information related to aviation. You can see our data repositories at https://github.com/open-air-data. Our mailing list is open to anyone.

ATC Radars

This map shows our collection of air traffic control radar locations. Knowing these locations makes it possible to locate aircraft using a technique known as "beamfinding". The raw data used to generate the map is in this github repository: https://github.com/open-air-data/atc-radar.
